Overview

Oxford Fine Dining, a sister company to Cotswold Fine Dining, partnered with Beyond Your Brand to redesign and rebuild its website. Their existing site was basic and didn’t effectively represent the brand’s identity or showcase their offerings.

My role was to design and develop a site that aligned with the company's luxury image. I focused on creating a clean, modern design that made the most of the client’s high-quality images, using plenty of white space to ensure the visuals were the focal point. I also incorporated subtle animations and parallax effects to add depth and interactivity without overwhelming the content.

The site’s new design, along with its enhanced functionality and streamlined structure, created a more intuitive and engaging user experience, making the site more distinctive and memorable. This ultimately strengthened the brand’s online presence.
