Overview

Beyond Your Brand was tasked with redesigning and rebuilding the website for the Royal Adelaide Hotel in Windsor to create a more modern, sophisticated online presence that would set it apart from other similar hotels. The previous site had clunky navigation and a flat gold colour scheme that made the site feel outdated.

My role was to design a site that showcased the hotel’s offerings. I used high-quality images throughout and added a video loop in the hero section to create a more dynamic experience. I also updated the gold colour scheme from a flat tone to a gradient, giving it a more refined and modern look.

The updated site features clearer layouts, making it easier for visitors to explore the hotel’s offerings, from room details to dining options. The design also had to be adaptable, as the owner wanted us to create a similar site for her other hotel in Eton using the same style.
