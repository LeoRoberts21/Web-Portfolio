Overview

Streamlined Analytics, a B2B software startup, approached Beyond Your Brand to design and develop a website that would establish a professional online presence and communicate its services. The client wanted a clean, user-friendly design with the flexibility to grow alongside their new business.

My role was to lead the design of a straightforward yet impactful website that would serve as a solid foundation for their brand. I created an intuitive, easy-to-navigate structure with clear descriptions of their core services. I also designed the layout to be flexible, allowing for future content and page expansions as the business grows.
