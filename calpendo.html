Website Design & development for a Facility Management Software Company.

Company
Calpendo by Exprodo

My Role
Designer & Developer

Project Aims

Calpendo, a software developed by Exprodo, approached us with a website that was clunky and cluttered with too many unnecessary pages. This made it difficult to navigate and understand the purpose of their software. My challenge was to redesign the site to provide a more professional online presence that catered to their target audience.

My primary goal was to clearly showcase what the software does. I achieved this by breaking down the services they offer, including screenshots of the software throughout the site, outlining its features, and presenting testimonials.

The design has been approved by the client, and we are now in the process of building the new site.
