Overview

Cotswold Fine Dining, a catering company serving the Cotswolds, approached us for a comprehensive website redesign and rebuild. This project was part of a broader effort for the same client, which also included designing a new site for their sister company, Oxford Fine Dining.

The client's existing website presented several challenges: it was difficult to navigate, featured disorganized menus, and lacked a cohesive identity. Our task was to transform the site into a polished, user-friendly platform that embodied the luxurious, high-end essence of Cotswold Fine Dining.

The final result was a sleek, visually appealing website that exceeded the client's expectations and established a stronger, more professional online presence for them.
